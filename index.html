The Problem

The Deadline Fighter Syndrome

Our collective research found students drowning in a Data Swamp spread across Canvas, Email, and WhatsApp. Deadlines are visible everywhere but coherent nowhere.

Canvas announcements and assignment streams
Email reminders and fragmented threads
WhatsApp social and logistics overload

Shared goal: lower cognitive load by separating High-Stress academic tasks from High-Joy personal life tasks through a minimalist interface.

Project Philosophy

From G1 Discovery to G2 Integration

Our philosophy is simple: understand student reality deeply in G1, then transform those insights into one coherent, low-friction product in G2.

G1 Mindset

Research Before Features

We treat deadlines, stress, and fragmented communication as a human problem before a technical one. This means listening first, mapping pain points, and grounding every design choice in observed behavior.

G2 Mindset

Unity Over Ownership

We do not ship isolated ideas. We synthesize each teammate's strongest contribution into a single, calm system so users experience clarity, not feature overload.
